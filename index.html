Status Stream lets you search Facebook for public status updates about, well, anything. You'll see realtime updates from people all over the world who are talking about whatever you're searching for.

You can search for one thing, or several things. Just add your search terms after the '#' in the URL - and separate each with a '|' (pipe) character.

Can't think of anything? try these:

  • The Simpsons OR Family Guy OR South Park
  • Barack Obama
  • Facebook
  • Love
  • London
  • Fail
  • I'm Listening to...
